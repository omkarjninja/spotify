Artography of music

The term "artography" generally refers to the process of creating visual art through photography or other media. However, in the context of music, "artography" may refer to the use of music to create visual art in a synesthetic experience In artography of music, artists may use various techniques to visualize the sounds and emotions conveyed by the music. This can include creating abstract or representational imagery, using light and color to evoke moods, or incorporating movement and choreography. Some musicians themselves also explore artography as a way to visually express their music. For example, musician Björk has incorporated elaborate music videos and stage shows into her performances that create a visual representation of her music. Overall, artography of music is a way to enhance the emotional impact and artistic expression of music by adding a visual component to the experience.
